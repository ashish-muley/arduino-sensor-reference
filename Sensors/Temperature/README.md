# Temperature Sensors

Sensors for measuring temperature (and often humidity). Listed roughly easiest → most capable.

| Sensor | Measures | Interface | Difficulty | Folder |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| LM35 | Temperature | Analog | Beginner | [LM35/](LM35/) |
| DHT11 | Temp + Humidity | Digital 1-wire | Beginner | [DHT11/](DHT11/) |
| DHT22 | Temp + Humidity | Digital 1-wire | Beginner | [DHT22/](DHT22/) |
| DS18B20 | Temperature | 1-Wire (Dallas) | Beginner | [DS18B20/](DS18B20/) |

**Higher-precision options** (SHT31-D, etc.) are documented in [Component-Reference](../../Component-Reference/README.md).
