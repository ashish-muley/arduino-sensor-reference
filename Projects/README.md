## Why Projects Come Last

Each project here is just the individual sensors from [`Sensors/`](../Sensors/) combined with what you've learned in [`Communication/`](../Communication/). Build the individual pieces first — trying to jump straight to a project without the fundamentals usually means more time debugging wiring than learning anything.
